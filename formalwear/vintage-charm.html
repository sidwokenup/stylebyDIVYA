The Divya Convertible Ensemble

The true beauty of the Divya Convertible Ensemble lies in its limitless styling potential, positioning it as the ultimate luxury investment piece.

  • Ensemble Components:
  • Wrap-Style Trouser: A flattering, high-waist trouser with an elegant drape.
  • Multi-Way Cape Top: The versatile hero piece that can transform its neckline and sleeve structure
  • White Tube Top (Inner): Provides coverage and a foundation for the more revealing top variations.
Price: 3590 INR

Light Care Instructions (Keep it White & Beautiful)

Item Quick Care Tips for Longevity
Cape Top & Trouser Hand Wash COLD or Dry Clean. Use gentle detergent. DO NOT tumble dry.
Tube Top (Inner) Machine Wash Cold (Delicates in a bag). Avoid bleach on main pieces; use on tube top for stains.
Drying Lay Flat to Dry (Air dry). Always air dry away from direct sun.
Ironing Low Heat (Inside out). Use a press cloth on the delicate cape top.
