Viraasat Varnika

Viraasat Varnika” is more than just an outfit — it’s a celebration of emotions, culture, and sustainability. This dress was lovingly created from my Mama’s saree, a symbol of warmth, tradition, and countless memories. With every fold of the skirt and every knot of the bow, I’ve preserved not just fabric — but feelings. It’s a reminder that beauty doesn’t always need something new — sometimes, it just needs the courage to reimagine the old. Wrapped in softness, adorned with grace, this piece tells the story of a girl embracing her womanhood with a modern silhouette and ancestral soul. Made sustainably, “Viraasat Varnika” reflects a promise — to stay rooted while blooming a new.

  • Handcrafted from vintage silk sarees
  • Sustainable fashion initiative
  • One-of-a-kind design
  • Traditional embroidery details
