Noor-e-Ruhani

Noor-e-Ruhani is a radiant expression of elegance and soulful heritage. Designed with a contemporary Indo-Western silhouette, this lehenga transitions gracefully from a soft beige at the waist to layers of blooming light pink, romantic rose, and smoky grey — capturing the journey of dawn to dusk in textile form. The one-shoulder navy blouse adds regal depth, while the flowing dupatta whispers tradition. Handcrafted sustainably, this piece symbolizes the revival of memories with every twirl — a divine light of grace reimagined.

  • Handcrafted from vintage silk sarees
  • Sustainable fashion initiative
  • One-of-a-kind design
  • Traditional embroidery details
