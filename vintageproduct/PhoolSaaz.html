PhoolSaaz

PhoolSaaz” is not just a dress — it’s a handcrafted story woven with care, culture, and conscious fashion. Born from an upcycled saree and dupatta, this Indo-western ensemble transforms the timeless elegance of florals into a statement of modern sustainability. With its pastel green flared skirt blooming in rose embroidery and the blush one-shoulder blouse wrapped like a petal, PhoolSaaz represents the gentle power of femininity — soft, expressive, yet unapologetically bold. Perfect for shoots, showcases, or slow fashion brands, this outfit is your answer to tradition kissed by trend, and a celebration of every woman who blooms with confidence.

  • Handcrafted from vintage silk sarees
  • Sustainable fashion initiative
  • One-of-a-kind design
  • Traditional embroidery details
